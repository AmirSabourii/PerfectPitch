# Sample Pitch Deck Content for Testing

## Sample 1: Strong Pitch (Expected Score: 75-85)

```
Slide 1: Problem
Small businesses lose $50B annually due to poor cash flow management. 82% of small business failures are due to cash flow problems. Current solutions are either too complex (enterprise software) or too simple (spreadsheets).

Slide 2: Solution
FlowAI is an AI-powered cash flow forecasting platform that predicts cash flow 90 days ahead with 95% accuracy. Simple interface, powerful predictions, actionable insights.

Slide 3: Market
- TAM: $12B (30M small businesses in US)
- SAM: $3B (businesses with $1M-$50M revenue)
- SOM: $300M (tech-forward SMBs in major metros)

Slide 4: Product
- Real-time bank integration
- AI forecasting engine (proprietary ML model)
- Automated alerts and recommendations
- Mobile-first design

Slide 5: Traction
- 500 beta users in 6 months
- $50K MRR, growing 25% MoM
- 40% conversion from free to paid
- NPS: 72

Slide 6: Business Model
- $99/month for basic plan
- $299/month for pro plan
- $799/month for enterprise
- Average LTV: $4,200
- CAC: $800 (payback in 3 months)

Slide 7: Competition
- QuickBooks: Too complex, not predictive
- Excel: Manual, error-prone
- Pulse: Limited features, no AI
- Our advantage: AI accuracy + simplicity

Slide 8: Team
- CEO: Ex-Stripe, built cash flow tools for 5 years
- CTO: Ex-Google, ML PhD from Stanford
- CFO: Ex-Goldman Sachs, 10 years SMB lending

Slide 9: Ask
Raising $2M seed round for:
- Product development (40%)
- Sales & marketing (40%)
- Team expansion (20%)
18-month runway to $1M ARR
```

Slide 3: Market
- 1M physicians in US
- Average spend: $10K/year on documentation tools
- $10B market opportunity

Slide 4: Product

- Auto-generates SOAP notes

- HIPAA compliant

Slide 5: Traction
- 50 doctors using beta
- Saves average 2 hours/day
- 90% would recommend
- Piloting with 2 hospital systems

Slide 6: Business Model
$500/month per doctor
Target: 1,000 doctors in year 1
$6M ARR potential

Slide 7: Competition

Slide 8: Team
